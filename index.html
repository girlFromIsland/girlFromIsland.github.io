  • Taipei 101

Taipei 101

Taipei 101 is the tallest building in Taiwan. This famous attraction has 101 stories. It used to be called the Taipei World Financial Center.

National Palace Museum

The National Palace Museum is the most famous museum in Taiwan. This museum houses over 700,000 pieces of Chinese artwork and historic artifacts, one of the largest collection in the world.

Kenting National Park

Kenting National Park is the oldest and the largest national park in Taiwan. This national park is well known for its beautiful beach, scenic mountain, and tropical climate.

Shilin Night Market

The Shilin Night Market is the largest and one of the most famous night market in Taiwan. This night market contains a food court, parking lot, roadside stands, cinemas, video arcades, and karaoke bar. Like most other night markets, Shilin Night Market opens at 16:00 and close at 02:00.

High-Heel Wedding Church

The High-Heel Wedding Church is a very famous piece of architecture in Taiwan is located in Chiayi County. Shaped like a high-heel and made of over 300 pieces of blue-tinted glass, this stunning architecture is a very famous place for holding weddings.
